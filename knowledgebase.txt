=== PASSWORD RESET INSTRUCTIONS ===
If you receive a message that your password has expired or needs to be changed, follow these steps:

1. Go to the company portal and submit a ticket for a password change.
2. Once your ticket is accepted, the onscreen instructions will appear to create a new password.
3. Type your new password and confirm it by retyping it.
4. After changing your password, lock your computer and sign in again using your new password.
5. If you cannot reset your password after the ticket is approved, contact the IT Helpdesk for further assistance.

Reminder:
Never share your password with anyone.
Password resets and access tickets are monitored for security and compliance.

=== PERMISSIONS / ACCESS REQUEST INSTRUCTIONS ===
If an app or file says, “Access denied” or “You don’t currently have permission to access this file,” follow these steps:

1. Double-check that you’re logged in with your work account.
2. If it’s a shared document, ask the file owner to confirm your access.
3. Go to the Company Portal > Access Requests > submit a form for permission.
4. Include in your form:
   - File or system name
   - Your department
   - Level of access needed (view or edit)
5. You’ll get an email once access is approved. Log out, then back in to apply changes.
6. If access is still denied after approval, contact the IT Helpdesk for troubleshooting.

=== WEBCAM & MICROPHONE TROUBLESHOOTING ===
If your camera or microphone is not working during video calls, follow these steps:

1. Make sure your webcam or headset is properly connected to your device.
2. Check if another application is already using the camera or microphone and close it.
3. Restart your video meeting app (Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet).
4. Within the app, go to Settings > Privacy > Camera/Microphone > Allow access.
5. Check your computer settings:
   - Go to Settings > Privacy > Camera/Microphone > Allow access
6. Test your camera or microphone using your device’s test tool.
7. If it still doesn’t work, submit a ticket to the IT Helpdesk and include:
   - The app name
   - Your device type (for example, laptop or desktop) or device name
   - A screenshot of the error (if possible)

=== HARDWARE & SOFTWARE REQUEST INSTRUCTIONS ===
If you need new equipment or software, follow these steps:

1. Go to the Company Portal > Request Center.
2. Choose either:
   - “Request Hardware” (for laptops, monitors, or headsets)
   - “Request Software” (for new applications or license renewals)
3. Complete the form with:
   - The item name
   - Business reason for the request
   - Manager approval (if required)
4. Once submitted, you’ll receive a confirmation email with your ticket number.
5. IT Helpdesk will process your request and notify you when it’s approved or shipped.
6. If you need to check the status, go to “My Requests” in the portal.

Instructions Reminder:
Only approved hardware and software may be installed on company devices for security compliance.

=== WIFI CONNECTION TROUBLESHOOTING ===
If you cannot connect to the company Wi-Fi, follow these steps:

1. Make sure Wi-Fi is turned on in your device settings.
2. Select the correct company network name from the list.
3. When prompted, enter your network username and password.
4. If you receive an error, click “Forget Network,” reconnect, and re-enter your credentials.
5. Check if other devices nearby can connect; if they cannot, the issue may be network-wide.
6. If the problem continues, restart your device and try again.
7. If you still cannot connect, submit a ticket to the IT Helpdesk and include:
   - Your device name
   - The Wi-Fi network you tried connecting to
   - Any error messages you saw
