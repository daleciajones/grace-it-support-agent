Wifi Connection Instructions: 
If you cannot connect to the company Wi-Fi, follow these steps: 

1. Make sure Wi-Fi is turned on in your device settings.
2. Select the correct company network name from the list. 
3. When prompted, enter your network username and password.
4. If you receive an error, click "Forget Network," reconnect, and re-enter your credentials. 
5. Check if other devices nearby can connect, if they cannot, the issue may be network-wide. 
6. If the problem continues, restart your device and try again. 
7. If you still cannot connect, submit a ticket to the IT Helpdesk and include: 
-Your device name 
-The Wi-Fi network you tried connecting to
-Any error messages you saw
