Hardware or Software Request Instructions:
If you need new equipment or software, follow these steps:

1. Go to the Company Portal > Request Center.
2. Choose either: 
-“Request Hardware” (for laptops, monitors, or headsets)
-“Request Software” (for new applications or license renewals)
3. Complete the form with: 
-The item name 
-Business reason for the request 
-Manager approval(if required)
4. Once submitted, you’ll receive a confirmation email with your ticket number.
5. IT Helpdesk will process your request and notify you when it’s approved or shipped.
6. If you need to check the status, go to “My Requests” in the portal.

Instructions Reminder: 
Only approved hardware and software may be installed on company devices for security compliance.
